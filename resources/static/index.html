About DriveTheVote

DriveTheVote is an initiative that aims to increase voter turnout by providing free rides to the polls for people who might not otherwise have access to transportation. By partnering with ride-sharing services like Uber and Lyft, DriveTheVote makes it easy for people to get to the polls and exercise their right to vote. This initiative is particularly important in areas where public transportation is limited or where voters may face other barriers to accessing polling locations. By removing transportation as a barrier, DriveTheVote is helping to ensure that everyone has an equal opportunity to participate in the democratic process.
